Infusca...

Is my goto name for anything gaming or coding related. I made up the name for my first main World of Warcraft character. She was an undead rogue and I was trying to find a word that meant dark, or black, or something of the sort. I found "infusco", which means to darken or obscure , in latin. I took it upon myself to create a 'feminine' version of it and changed the "o" an "a".
