An explanation of my pseudonames (under image).


Infusca is my goto name for anything gaming or coding related. I made up the name for my first main World of Warcraft character. She was an undead rogue and I was trying to find a word that meant dark, or black, or something of the sort. I found "infusco", which means to darken or obscure , in latin. I took it upon myself to create a 'feminine' version of it and changed the "o" an "a".


Pickle was something my sister used to call me sometimes, when we were kids.


Wolfpet4me was my original username when internet first became a thing, or when we first got AOL. I still miss the days of going into the weather chatroom before school and talking to the adults in there. It was my favorite chatroom, and I still wonder about the people I met in there.


In regards to the Mario comment, when my sister and I used to play super mario brothers on SNES, she was always Mario, and I was always Luigi.
