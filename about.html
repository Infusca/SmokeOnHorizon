Infusca...


is the name I made up for my first main World of Warcraft character, back in college. She is an undead rogue. I wanted to find a word that meant dark, or black, or something of the sort. I found "infusco", which means to darken or obscure , in latin. I took it upon myself to create a 'feminine' version of it and changed the "o" an "a".
